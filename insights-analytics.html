Market Landscape Reporting

Problem / Opportunity:

Shimano serves global markets.

Keeping abreast of how the bicycle landscape is evolving is of key interest to stakeholders.


Action:

I plan and execute research trips to key markets, reporting on insights and recommended actions.

Client Discovery Interviews

Problem / Opportunity:

As a primary B2B business, Bike brands, OEMs and Dealers are key leads for Shimano’s RnD endeavours.

A high-yielding, repeatable process of extracting insights is of strategic value.


Action:

I hold collaborative interviews with customers, surfacing critical problems they are trying to solve.

Research insights help feed healthy feedback loops that contribute to constant improvement of our product-solutions.
