Developing A New Product Innovation Framework

Problem / Opportunity:

Small, incremental improvements with each update are a tried and tested formula. A challenge was put forth to discover and deliver outsized, out-of-box ideas.


Action:

I authored a 5-step workflow to guide engineering teams to:
  • scope projects
  • perform data collection
  • analyse and decide
  • prototype
  • test and deliver new product concepts

Referenced Design-Thinking Resources

2. Christensen, Clayton M., Taddy Hall, Karen Dillon, and David S. Duncan. 2016. Competing Against Luck: The Story of Innovation and Customer Choice. HarperBusiness.
